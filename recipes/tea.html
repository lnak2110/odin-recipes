Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Pour water into a mug. Add honey and heat in the microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  2. Stir in lemon juice, mixing until honey is dissolved, then stir in the sugar.
